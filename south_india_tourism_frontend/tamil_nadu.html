Tamil Nadu, located in the southernmost part of India, is known for its ancient temples, classical arts, and rich cultural heritage.

Tamil Nadu has a rich history dating back to the ancient Chola, Chera, and Pandya dynasties.

The state is renowned for its classical dance form Bharatanatyam, Carnatic music, and vibrant festivals.

Cultural Safety Tips

  • Respect temple dress codes and remove footwear before entering.
  • Avoid public displays of affection, especially in religious places.

Tamil Nadu experiences a tropical climate with distinct monsoon, summer, and winter seasons.

The best time to visit is between November and February when the weather is cooler.

General Safety Tips
